## üìä Analysis Components

### 1. Categorical Insights
- **BRId**: BRId 3 dominates; BRId 2 & 4 least frequent  
- **GenderId**: Balanced distribution  
- **Credit Cards**: Majority own 1 card  
- **Nationality**: Europeans & Asians dominate; Africans least represented  
- **Fee Structure**: High fee structure most common  
- **Loyalty Classification**: Jade most common; Platinum least  
- **Risk Weighting**: Risk 2 most frequent  
- **Income Band**: Medium income band dominates  

### 2. Numerical Insights
- All financial variables (Income, Savings, Deposits, Loans, Accounts) show **right-skewed distributions**.  
- Wealth concentration among a small group; majority hold lower balances.  

### 3. Scatter Plot Insights
- **Business Lending vs Bank Loans**: Strong positive correlation  
- **Bank Loans vs Credit Card Balance**: Higher loans linked to higher balances  
- **Income vs Checking Accounts**: Positive trend with variability  
- **Age vs Superannuation Savings**: Slight negative correlation (withdrawal phase)  
- **Checking vs Saving Accounts**: Strong positive correlation  

### 4. Correlation Matrix
- **Bank Deposits & Checking Accounts**: 0.84 (strong)  
- **Bank Deposits & Saving Accounts**: 0.75 (strong)  
- **Bank Loans & Business Lending**: 0.42 (moderate)
